More Than Just Dry Cleaning

At The Dhobi Station, each garment is individually evaluated to determine the safest and most effective method of cleaning.
For many high-end and delicate items, drycleaning is the best option to clean and restore them.
However depending on the type of stain, and the material your clothing is made out of wetcleaning and handwashing are both excellent solutions.
For optimal results, each piece is uniquely treated to remove a wide variety of stains, which include grease, lipstick, ink, blood, food, coffee, wine and even toothpaste!
